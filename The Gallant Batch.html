The Gallant Batch

Empowering women, especially in a country like India has become a necessity. From the day we are born, we are just compared to other girls and mostly in terms of beauty. As a kid, I used to think that a girl's importance depended on her beauty. Isn't it sad? We only see movies where a princess is saved by a prince. We are never told the stories of how to fight for each other, instead of against each other. The Gallant Batch is a magazine that captures the power of women-driven strikes from Indian history. I did the whole branding and editorial design for it.

SOFTWARE USED

Adobe Photoshop
Cinema 4D
Adobe After Effects

Strategy

The Gallant Batch is a newly launched magazine about women's empowerment.
The problem is that most women degrade each other as they feel insecure about themselves.
However, those women are never taught how to stand up and fight for other women.
We need to allow women to realize the power of sisterhood
so that women no longer feel insecure about other women
helping to empower women through the magazine.

Brand Guidelines

Theme- Elegantly Bold (Many people think that elegance is a woman's weakness, rather it is their strength.
Tone Of Voice- Elegant, Modern, United, and Futuristic
Logo- I have extended the lines of the T and G alphabet to cover words as an umbrella to show how women look bold together.
Type- I chose Didonesque Roman as the primary font as its contrast in strokes and x-height gives an elegant yet bold look. Josefin Slab as the secondary font skillfully balances it, and its sharp edges give the view of a constrained world where women live.
Color Palette- I used black and white to give a modern contrast. I deliberately used pink to show how powerful can this color be, if it is accompanied by other strong colors. Lastly, I used a tint of neon green to add a more powerful and engaging vibe to the magazine
3d Experiments- Lastly, I learnt and experimented a lot on Cinema 4D. I hope you like it ;)
